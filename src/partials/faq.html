FAQ

  • How do I start playing Nuts & Bolts Sort Puzzle?

    Download the game from the Play Store and start sorting nuts and bolts by color and type.

  • Is the game free?

    Yes, the game is free with optional in-app purchases for extra hints and power-ups.

  • Can I play without an internet connection?

    Yes, Nuts & Bolts Sort Puzzle offers offline play for uninterrupted fun.

  • How often are new levels added?

    New levels and puzzles are added regularly to keep the experience fresh.

  • Are there any in-game rewards?

    Yes, you can earn hints and power-ups by completing levels and challenges.
